Conclusion:¶

1) The highest number of buyers are Mainstream-young singles/couples, then Mainstream-retirees and Budget-OLDER FAMILIES follows.

2) Older families and young families in general buy more chips per customer.

3) Mainstream-young singles and couples are more willing to pay more per packet of chips compared to their budget and premium counterparts.

4) Mainstream-young singles/couples are more likely to purchase Tyrrells, Twisties, and Doritos chips compared to other brands.

5) Mainstream-singles/couples are more likely to purchase a 270g and 380g pack sizes compared to other pack sizes.

6) Mainstream-young/couples are more likely to purchase Twisties-270g and Doritos-380g chips.
